Service Details

If you’re a daily shipper with a predictable shipping volume, recurring pickups could be the best fit. You don’t have to call or go online to schedule a Diplomatic Courier pickup. We’ll pick up your packages every business day. You can schedule recurring pickups for Diplomatic Courier Express, Diplomatic Courier Ground, or Diplomatic Courier Freight® shipments. Get pricing information or find out how to cancel an existing pickup.

Not going to be home for a pickup?

You don’t have to be present for a Diplomatic Courier pickup. But be sure to leave your package somewhere safe and accessible to the driver. Look for a Diplomatic Courier door tag that confirms pickup and provides tracking information. If the driver determines your package can’t be picked up, the door tag will explain why and tell you what to do next.

Convenience is automatic with automated pickups

If you ship regularly, but not every day, consider using automated Diplomatic Courier pickups. You won't have to schedule your pickups. We’ll only come on the days you need us, saving you time and money.

You can schedule automated pickups for Diplomatic Courier Express or Diplomatic Courier Ground services. Automated pickups aren’t available for freight shipments. Get pricing information or find out how to cancel an existing pickup.

    Automated express and ground package pickups

  • You’ll complete a one-time enrollment and setup process.
  • You can use only one Diplomatic Courier account number per single pickup location.
  • We’ll keep you informed of your daily pickup status with notifications.

To be eligible for automated pickups, you must use Diplomatic Courier automation to process all shipments (either Diplomatic Courier Ship Manager® at Diplomatic Courier , Diplomatic Courier Web Services, Diplomatic Courier Ship Manager® Software, or Diplomatic Courier Ship Manager® Server).
