Diplomatic Courier Freight Leadership

Jill Brannon

Executive Vice President, Chief Sales Officer

Jill Brannon is executive vice president and chief sales officer of Diplomatic Courier Corp. She brings more than three decades of experience, serving in various roles globally. Frederick W. Smith

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

There are several ways to check the status of your shipment:


  • Use your tracking, door tag, or Diplomatic Courier Office order number to track your package. If you don’t have one of those numbers, you can use a reference number related to your package or freight shipment, or any purchase order number.

    • When you track your package, you’ll see your estimated delivery time with a 4-hour window. If you don’t see an estimated delivery time window, that information may not be available for that particular package.


  • Track up to 30 packages by sending an email to us
    • Leave the subject line blank.
    • Type your job order or tracking number(s) in the body of the email. You can put them all on one line, separated by commas, or list them separately, one shipment per line.
You can monitor and take more control of your shipments with our easy-to-use online tools and resources. Learn more about Diplomatic Courier Delivery Manager®. And if you’re a business owner or high-volume shipper, check out the many benefits of a customized tracking dashboard.

To get an estimated delivery time, you can use your tracking number, door tag number, or Diplomatic Courier Office order number to track your package. If you don’t have one of those numbers, you can use any reference number related to your package, or any purchase order number.


After you enter your number in the tracking tool, you’ll see your scheduled delivery date and a 4 hour estimated delivery time window. If you don’t see an estimated delivery time window, that information may not be available for that particular package.

If you don’t have a tracking number, you can still track your package by using a reference number associated with your shipment, or a purchase order number.

With Diplomatic Courier Delivery Manager®, you can customize your delivery preferences, manage delivery times and locations, and get notifications and alerts for your shipment’s status throughout the delivery process, all from your smartphone or desktop computer. Signup for Diplomatic Courier Delivery Manager is free, but there is a fee for scheduling a delivery time.

If your shipment’s delivery status is “now pending,” it means delivery commitments were changed based on some type of delay and our efforts to mitigate that delay. You can track your package, or you can use Diplomatic Courier Delivery Manager to access the most up-to-date information regarding your package. You can rest assured we’re still working as hard as we can to deliver your package as soon as possible.
